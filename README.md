## Overview
XYZ Company has been experiencing a consistent employee attrition rate of around 15% over the past few years, negatively impacting productivity and organizational stability. This project focuses on analyzing employee data to identify the key factors contributing to attrition and presenting insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

In this project, I played the role of an HR Analyst, using data visualization and analytics to support strategic decision-making for employee retention.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition patterns across different demographics and job roles  
- Identify key factors influencing employee turnover  
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for HR decision-makers  
- Provide actionable insights to reduce attrition and improve employee satisfaction  

---

## Key Metrics Analyzed
- Attrition by Age  
- Attrition by Education Field  
- Attrition by Years at Company  
- Employee Count by Business Travel  
- Monthly Income by Job Role  
- Monthly Income by Department  
- Job Role vs Job Satisfaction
